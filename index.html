Part-Time Programming Course

Epicodus offers a part-time version of the Introduction to Programming course. This course covers the fundamentals of web design and web development. Upon completing the course, you may continue into the full-time program if you wish. We currently do not offer Part-Time Courses for other Tracks such as Ruby/Rails, Java/Android, and CSS/Design. However, in the Part-Time Course, we do offer basic fundamentals of HTML and CSS.
